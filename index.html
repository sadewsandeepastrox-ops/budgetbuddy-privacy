ACCOUNT DELETION
How to Delete Your Account
Your account and all associated data can be permanently deleted directly inside the app.
  1. Open BudgetBuddy on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Enter your password to confirm, then tap Delete My Account.

This permanently deletes your Firebase authentication account and all locally stored data including transactions, budgets, goals, loans, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services

BudgetBuddy uses the following Google services, each governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Firebase Authentication — stores your email and manages login
  • Firebase Crashlytics — anonymous crash reporting to help fix bugs
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage data to improve the app
  • Google Play Billing — handles premium purchases securely

Data Security

All communication with Firebase is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Your financial data (transactions, budgets, goals) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

BudgetBuddy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
